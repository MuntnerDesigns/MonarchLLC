OUR STORY

From our very humble beginnings in the early 2000's, our team has grown into one of the largest privately held protein companies in the food industry. Our ability to supply the highest quality meat & seafood proteins is built on the backbone of our ethos, source responsible the highest quality meat & seafood items that extend well past the plate, while leaving a lasting positive impact within the communities we partner with, empower their stories while reinvesting back into those communities. We continue to invest and acquire assets while remaining a humble team to support national level food service & retail programs across all meat & seafood proteins.

Distributing the finest wholesale beef, pork, lamb, mutton, poultry, and seafood from vendors all across the U.S. and overseas. Contact us online or call (323) 374-0303.
