THE MONARCH PROMISE

100% TRACEABLE & TRANSPARENT

From the water to your table, our commitment to partner with the team at Whole Chain (TM) allows us to know every movement of our unique supply chain. We begin with the purest form of seafood from harvest and we end with the most trusted partners brining you the highest quality seafood from around the world.

SUSTAINABILITY
ETHOS

We have built our legacy on the richness of the resource, and we pledge to partner with industry leaders such as ASC, BAP, MSC, Seafood Watch, and others that have established rigorous standards & certifications that encompass the lifecycle of the fishery. We abide by these regulations and hold our supply chain to standards second to none, and we will never compromise the sustainability of the resource.

BETTER BUSINESS
PLEDGE

As an active member of the National Fisheries Institute, we pledge to assure we abide by industry standards that demonstrate our leadership position and holding ourselves and our industry accountable under the NFI's "Better Business" pledge.

SOCIAL & SDG LEADERSHIP

Our team has spent years scouring the waters to bring the freshest seafood sources from around the world responsibly. These searches have allowed us to partner with industry leaders such as Fair Trade to bring socially conscious seafood products to shelves & tables everywhere. We continue to level-up our social commitment empowering the stories of the seas & waters to make measurable differences in communities we have footprints within. We believe every decision we take can have a positive & lasting social impact.
